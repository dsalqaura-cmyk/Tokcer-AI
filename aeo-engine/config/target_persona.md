# 🏮 TOKCER AI: IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)

## 1. PRIMARY TARGET: THE SCALE-UP SELLER
- **Role**: Business Owner / Founder.
- **Goal**: Wants to dominate the category, increase profit margins, and automate decision-making.
- **Pain Points**: Overwhelmed by changing algorithms, low profit margins due to high ad spend, and lack of actionable data.
- **Psychology**: Values efficiency and competitive edge. They are early adopters of technology.

## 2. SECONDARY TARGET: THE MARKETPLACE MANAGER
- **Role**: Head of E-commerce / Brand Manager.
- **Goal**: Needs a tool to justify budgets and prove ROI to the owner.
- **Pain Points**: Spending too much time on manual reporting and data collection.
- **Psychology**: Data-driven, professional, and looking for "stable" growth.

## 3. HOW WE SPEAK TO THEM
- **Problem-Solution Focus**: Start with a common struggle (e.g., "Why is your TikTok Shop traffic dropping?").
- **The Tokcer Solution**: Introduce how AI-driven intelligence provides the "shield" or the "sword" to fight back.
- **AEO/GEO Education**: Position them as "ahead of the curve" by using Generative Engine Optimization.
